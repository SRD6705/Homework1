All About Stephen D

Originally from chicago, I moved to NY in 2002 for graduate school. In 2004 I graduated from LIU CW Post with a Masters in Clinical Art Therapy. I was maried in 2010 and in 2011 my wife and I had our first child, Indigo.

stephen dickens website

As an artist, I am really intersted in the idea of non-objective elements (familiar shapes and lines) merging or colliding with objective elements, such as familiar building-like structures.

  • My favorite figuritive painter is, Francis Bacon

    • Francis Bacon wasa British painter who had a tremendous amount of tension in his paintings, but formally they were incredibly balanced.

  • My favorite non-objective painter is, Wassily Kandinsky

    • Wassily Kandinsky was incredibly influential to my work as a painter.
